Loadout
  • MacBook Air M1, my beloved
    Spends half of its life as a real, laptopy laptop. The other half of the time, it tends to be accompanied by:
  • Dell S2240L
    Okay, it's a shitty monitor. But it has an IPS panel and a ✨glossy✨ finish, so the colors are decent! And at around 100 DPI, it works just right for non-retina viewing. On the rare occasion that a developer still cares about pixel-perfect graphics nowadays, it's pretty glorious!
  • Logitech K750
    One of the very few cheap, full-size Mac keyboards. I actually got it for 25€ from a wonderful person on eBay. It has this bizarre solar-charging function I've never seen anywhere else, but hey, it seems to work so far! All-in-all, I really like this thing.
  • Logitech M325
    What can I say, it's a… mouse. I basically grew up with one of these (and I really mean one, they're crazy reliable) so when looking for a new mouse, I had no better idea than just buy it again. Big fan of the fast scrolling wheel! (Miss me with that laggy, stepper nonsense.)
